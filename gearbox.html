Gearbox Rebuilding and Repair

Gearboxes are the heart of your drive systems—and when they fail, production comes to a halt. At Control Core, we specialize in restoring gearbox performance through comprehensive rebuilding and repair services.

Our process includes:

  • Complete disassembly and thorough inspection
  • Replacement of damaged or worn gears, shafts, and seals
  • Precision reassembly with proper torque specifications
  • Lubrication, leak testing, and performance validation

Whether it’s routine wear or catastrophic failure, we bring gearboxes back to OEM-level function with precision and reliability.
