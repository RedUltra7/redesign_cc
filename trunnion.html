Trunnion Rebuilding and Repair

When your trunnion bearings or housing start to show wear, precision rebuilding is essential to restore accuracy and reduce equipment stress. Control Core delivers high-quality trunnion repair with minimal downtime.

Services include:

  • Complete disassembly and inspection of trunnion components
  • Precision machining of worn or distorted surfaces
  • Replacement of bushings, bearings, and seals
  • Realignment and testing to ensure smooth, stable rotation

Our rebuilds are done with exacting standards and certified quality checks, so your equipment is fully restored and ready for production.
